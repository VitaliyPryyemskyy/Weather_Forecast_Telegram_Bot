If you're the only one at the party without a drink in your hand, you could feel left out. Having a non-alcoholic margarita to cheer with at a party is much more festive! Many feel uplifted when consuming zero-proof cocktails because they know they will stay sharp all night, and wake up feeling refreshed.

Studies show that drinking non-alcoholic beer can reduce your risk of heart disease, aid bone growth and even reduce your chance of getting illnesses like the common cold. And that's not all; many zero-alcohol drinks contain vitamins and minerals including folic acid, potassium, iron and zinc.

Virgin Margarita

<<===Click on picture to see recipe

A margarita with no alcohol is made with fresh citrus juices, agave nectar, and topped with sparkling water. Served on the rocks, this virgin cocktail is perfect for sipping poolside, as a refreshing treat at a hot summer barbecue, or when you want to shake things up on taco Tuesday. It can even be made with more fun flavors or blended with ice if you want!

Appletini Mocktail

<<===Click on picture to see recipe

People looking for a non-alcoholic choice love the Appletini Mocktail because of its adaptability and delicious flavor. This mocktail, made with apple juice, zesty lemon juice, and a splash of Granny Smith apple syrup, is one of the finest virgin drinks because of the pleasant explosion of fruity and citrus tastes it provides. Raise your glass, take a drink, and let the Appletini Mocktail’s exquisite tastes whisk you away to an apple-infused paradise.
