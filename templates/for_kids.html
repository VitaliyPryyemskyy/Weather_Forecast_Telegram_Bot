As a bartender, it’s important to know how to make drinks for all ages. And while many of us may think that kids only drink soda or juice, there are actually a wide variety of non-alcoholic drinks that are perfect for children.

In fact, a well-made kids cocktail can be just as delicious and satisfying as any adult beverage. But what makes a great kids cocktail? For starters, it should be visually appealing and fun to drink. Bright colors, interesting textures, and playful garnishes can all add to the experience.

Shirley Temple

<<===Click on picture to see recipe

A Shirley Temple is a non-alcoholic mixed drink. It is sometimes served to children who are dining with adults. People do not know if this drink was actually served to the child actress Shirley Temple or if it was just named after her. We might not know the whole story, but we do know that Shirley Temples are a kid-favorite!
